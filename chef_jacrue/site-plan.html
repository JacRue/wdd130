Audience

People with health goals looking for professional made meals, people looking for personalised dinning experiance to meet their needs for events, parties, family dinning and romantic settings. Parents with busy lives that need a little help or a night off in the kitchen or anyone who would like to have a professional chef teach them on cooking techniques and meals. The site must be available on mobile platforms, desktops, laptops, possible app. would be good. The site needs to be easy to use/understand, meal plan examples and photos.

Content

Home page

• Why will people come to your site? Why this website, this website will provide information for services that provide professional cooking experiences, professional cooking tips/classes, and food events. People wanting to experience culinary services elevated beyond their normal experiences, the opportunity to have planned dinners or events to impress loved ones or clients for the company that wants to impress. The website will also be for someone wanting to learn culinary tricks/tips without the culinary school bill attached. Maybe your someone who is a foodie and wants to have certain cuisine prepared for you that your local restaurants don’t know or they are unable to provide, having a personal chef can make those foodie dreams possible. The website is also for those who have health goals/needs but doesn’t have the time to prepare such intercut meal preparations, having a personal chef can take all the worry out of your meal prep for you, so you can focus on the other more demanding things in your lives. Information about foods, spices, drinks and more, what equipment chefs use to create such amazing dishes. Postings on social media about the chef and updates on what’s trending. • What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? What types of services are offered, the costs of said services, reservation dates, times of services offered, waiting list (if needed), locations of services offered or willing to travel to, payment options, packages offered for services, guest limits, cancelations and the clients responsibilities, insurance information and credentials, social network information, and FAQ.

Why this site

• Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? This website will be easy to use and understand, clean and thoughtful. Here in Houston Texas, there are not many professional chefs offering all the services this website offers, this will be a great advantage for the client to have access to. This website will also provide information about local food events, meet and greets in the foodie world, recommendations for other great restaurants to try outside of the personal chef experience. Information about foods, spices, drinks, and where to find them. Postings from the chef on social media about events, updates on new cuisines and what’s trending in the foodie world. This website is not just for the average joe who likes food, this website is for foodies, people who want a personal chef experience to celebrate food with family, friends, clients and coworkers alike. Someone looking to be emersed in the foodie world, where they have a one stop shop to find what’s happing in the local Houston Texas seen and more. This website is a celebration of cuisine…
