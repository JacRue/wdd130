Why will people come to your site? Why this website, this website will provide information for services that provide professional cooking experiences, professional cooking tips/classes, and food events. People wanting to experience culinary services elevated beyond their normal experiences, the opportunity to have planned dinners or events to impress loved ones or clients for the company that wants to impress. The website will also be for someone wanting to learn culinary tricks/tips without the culinary school bill attached. Maybe your someone who is a foodie and wants to have certain cuisine prepared for you that your local restaurants don’t know or they are unable to provide, having a personal chef can make those foodie dreams possible. The website is also for those who have health goals/needs but doesn’t have the time to prepare such intercut meal preparations, having a personal chef can take all the worry out of your meal prep for you, so you can focus on the other more demanding things in your lives. Information about foods, spices, drinks and more, what equipment chefs use to create such amazing dishes. Postings on social media about the chef and updates on what’s trending.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? What types of services are offered, the costs of said services, reservation dates, times of services offered, waiting list (if needed), locations of services offered or willing to travel to, payment options, packages offered for services, guest limits, cancelations and the clients responsibilities, insurance information and credentials, social network information, and FAQ.
